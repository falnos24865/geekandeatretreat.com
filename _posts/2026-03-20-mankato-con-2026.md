Today marks the beginning of something special in southern Minnesota— [Mankato Con 2026](https://www.mankatocon.com/) has officially begun! Running from March 20 - 22 at the Mayo Clinic Health System Event Center in Mankato, this brand-new convention is already bringing together gamers, creators, and curious newcomers for a full weekend of tabletop fun.

As the inaugural year of the event, there’s a buzz of excitement in the air. From open gaming tables and a demo library to tournaments and scheduled sessions, there’s something happening around every corner. Whether you’re into board games, RPGs, trading card games, or miniatures, this con is built to welcome everyone from seasoned strategists to first-time players.

One of the highlights already underway is a packed schedule of tournaments, including fan-favorite miniature battles like Warhammer events and other competitive play opportunities. It’s not just about competition, it’s about community.

Even better, Mankato Con isn’t just fun, it’s meaningful. Proceeds from the event go toward supporting the Minnesota Valley Action Council (MVAC), helping provide essential services to local communities. So every dice roll and card draw contributes to a good cause.

If you’re in or near Mankato this weekend, it’s not too late to jump in, meet fellow gamers, and be part of the very first chapter of what could become a lasting tradition.

Game on, Mankato
